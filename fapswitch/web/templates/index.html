Webswitch MOF generator

Fapswitch is a method to generate MOFs as functionalised derivatives of known MOFs. For every visit to this page, a new MOF will be generated randomly from billions of combinations of functional group modifications of known MOFs.

More information can be found in the publication: In preparation.
